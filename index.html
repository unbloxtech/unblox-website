What is unblox?

This revolutionary new platform will allow you to create, issue, and verify document, certificate or anything digital in a secure, decentralized, and simple manner. Built on Archethic Blockchain technology, unblox provides increased security, ease of use, and instant verification, making the process of creation & verification quick and stress-free.

With unblox, you can say goodbye to the days of waiting for verification. Our API is user-friendly and highly customizable, making it easy for organizations and institutions to integrate unblox into their existing systems.
  • What problem is unblox solving?
    The problem that unblox is solving is the inefficiency and unreliability of traditional document verification methods. Currently, verifying the authenticity of a document or certificate can take several days and is often a cumbersome process. This not only wastes time but also puts individuals and businesses at risk of accepting fraudulent documents.

    This is a problem worth solving because a reliable and efficient method of document verification is crucial in today's fast-paced world. From education to business, the authenticity of documents plays a vital role in many aspects of daily life. Verifying the validity of a document quickly and securely can help prevent fraud and save valuable time, making it an important issue to address.

    unblox solves this problem by leveraging the power of blockchain technology to provide a secure and decentralized platform for document verification. With its all-in-one solution and user-friendly API, unblox makes it easy for organizations to create, issue, and verify their own documents, allowing for quick and reliable verification with just a simple scan of a QR code.
  • Why unblox is in the form of an API?
    unblox addresses the problem of unreliable and inefficient digital verification by providing a secure and decentralized platform for digital management. The solution is in the form of an API for several reasons:

    All-in-One Solution: The unblox API provides an all-in-one solution for companies, universities, and institutions to create, issue, and verify their own documents. This makes the process of document management streamlined and more efficient.

    Decentralization: By using Archethic blockchain technology, unblox offers a decentralized platform for document verification. This ensures that the verification process is secure and tamper-proof, with no single point of failure.

    User-Friendly: The unblox API is designed to be user-friendly, making it easy for organizations to integrate the platform into their existing systems. The API eliminates the need for complex and technical processes, making document management simple and accessible.

    Instant Verification: With the integration of QR codes, unblox provides instant verification of documents with just a simple scan. This eliminates the need for lengthy and time-consuming verification processes, saving valuable time for both individuals and organizations.
